Meet The CEO

Robert Pankey has been the CEO of Bert Petroleum Consultants since November 2009. He believes that safe operations, commercial innovation and technological innovation are the cornerstones needed to deliver better value for operators, employees and owners. Robert joined Vesco Oil Corporation in 2016 and his career in offshore drilling spans a number of technical, operational and leadership roles. These include Rig Manager, Operations Manager and more recently Chief Operating Officer here in United States. Robert holds a Master’s degree in Science in Engineering from the Ivano Frankivsk State University of Oil & Gas and an MBA from IMD and the University of Geneva.
